About This Test Page

This second page exists so you can verify that consent decisions and cookies persist as you navigate between pages — a key behaviour of cookie-consent SDKs. Accept or reject on the banner, then move between Home, Components, and About and watch the cookie panel.

Test checklist

  • Consent banner appears on first visit
  • Cookies are blocked before consent
  • Accepting sets the expected cookies
  • Choice persists across pages & reloads
